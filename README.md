# 📊 Tailwind Trades Power BI Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end data analysis and visualization of the Tailwind Traders dataset using Power BI.

The objective is to uncover insights related to:
- Revenue performance
- Product efficiency
- Market behavior
- Tax impact on revenue

⚠️ Note: The dataset does not include operational costs, so the analysis focuses on **net revenue efficiency** rather than profit.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Normalize multi-currency sales into USD
- Analyze revenue distribution across countries
- Identify top-performing products
- Evaluate revenue efficiency (Net Revenue %)
- Compare markets using profitability vs order value
- Build an interactive and business-ready dashboard

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI**
- **DAX (Time Intelligence, Aggregations)**
- **Power Query**
- **Excel**
- **Data Modeling**

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Sales data (Revenue, Tax, Products)
- Country mapping
- Purchase & supplier data

### Key Fields Used:
- Gross Revenue
- Net Revenue
- Total Tax
- Quantity Purchased
- Country

## 📊 Key Metrics

👉 Insight:

- Indicates **low tax impact (~7%)**

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🌍 Revenue Distribution
- UAE contributes **~58% of total revenue**
- UK and France are secondary contributors
- Australia has minimal contribution

---

### 📦 Product Performance
- Top product: **Modular Sofa Set (~93K revenue)**
- Revenue is concentrated among a few high-performing products
- Indicates strong product dependency

---

### 📊 Revenue Efficiency
- Net Revenue % remains stable (~93%) across months
- Suggests consistent tax structure
- No major fluctuations in revenue retention

---

### 📍 Market Analysis (Scatter Plot)
- UAE shows **high revenue & high order value**
- Some markets have:
  - Low revenue but stable order value
- Helps identify:
  - High-value vs high-volume markets

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1️⃣ Diversify Revenue Sources
- Heavy dependency on UAE market
- Expand focus on UK and France

---

### 2️⃣ Focus on High-Performing Products
- Promote top products (e.g., Modular Sofa Set)
- Optimize inventory and marketing for these

---

### 3️⃣ Explore Low-Performing Markets
- Investigate Australia & USA performance
- Improve pricing or logistics strategy

---

### 4️⃣ Improve Product Distribution
- Reduce dependency on a few products
